Category Moderation:

-----------------------------------------------------

+ban <member> [reason] || Bans a member from the server

-----------------------------------------------------

+unban <member> || Unbans a member

-----------------------------------------------------

+kick <member> [reason] || Kicks a member from the server

-----------------------------------------------------

+mute <member> [time] [reason] || Mutes a member

-----------------------------------------------------

+unmute <member> || Unmutes a member

-----------------------------------------------------

+clear <amount> || Deletes the amount of messages

-----------------------------------------------------

[] means optional argument and <> means required argument

Category Music:

-----------------------------------------------------

+summon [voice channel] || Summons the bot to any voice channel

-----------------------------------------------------

+join || Joins the voice channel u are currently in

-----------------------------------------------------

+leave || Leaves the voice channel

-----------------------------------------------------

+volume <volume> || Sets the volume of the player

-----------------------------------------------------

+now || Displays the currently playing song

-----------------------------------------------------

+pause || Pauses the currently playing song

-----------------------------------------------------

+resume || Resumes the currently playing song

-----------------------------------------------------

+stop || Stops playing the current song and clears the queue

-----------------------------------------------------

+skip || Vote to skip a song

-----------------------------------------------------

+queue || Shows the player's queue

-----------------------------------------------------

+shuffle || Shuffles the queue

-----------------------------------------------------

+remove <index> || Removes a song from the queue at a given index

-----------------------------------------------------

+loop || Loops the currently playing song, invoke this command again to unloop the song

-----------------------------------------------------

+play <video url/name> || Plays a song

-----------------------------------------------------

[] means optional argument and <> means required argument
