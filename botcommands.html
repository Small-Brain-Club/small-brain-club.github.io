Category Moderation:

+ban [member] [reason] || Bans a member from the server

+unban [member] || Unbans a member

+kick [member] [reason] || Kicks a member from the server

+mute [member] [reason] || Mutes a member

+unmute [member] || Unmutes a member

+clear [amount] || Deletes the amount of messages

Category Fun:

+meme || Sends a random meme

+dankmeme || Sends a random dankmeme

+joke || Tells a random joke

+quote || Tells a random quote

Category Utility:

+calculate [number] [operation] [number] || Replaces your mathematical skills

+reminder [what u want to be reminded about] [time] || Reminds you about something

+wiki [request] || Searches wikipedia

+youtube [request] || Searches youtube

Category Info:

+userinfo [user] || Tells information about the user

+serverinfo || Tells information about the server

+ping || Tells the current ping of the bot

+uptime || Tells how long the bot has been online

Category Games:

+rps || Rock, paper, scissors

+rolldice || Rolls the dice

+trivia [num of questions] [difficulty] [category] || Gets random trivia questions

Category Music:

+summon [voice channel] || Summons the bot to any voice channel

+join || Joins the voice channel u are currently in

+leave || Leaves the voice channel

+volume [number] || Sets the volume of the player

+now || Displays the currently playing song

+pause || Pauses the currently playing song

+resume || Resumes the currently playing song

+stop || Stops playing the current song and clears the queue

+skip || Vote to skip a song

+queue || Shows the player's queue

+shuffle || Shuffles the queue

+remove [index] || Removes a song from the queue at a given index

+loop || Loops the currently playing song, invoke this command again to unloop the song

+play [song name/url] || Plays a video
