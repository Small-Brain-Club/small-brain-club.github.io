Music

+summon [voice channel] || Summons the bot to any voice channel

+join || Joins the current voice channel

+leave || Leaves the current voice channel

+play [song_url] || Plays a song

+volume [volume%] || Changes the volume of the player

+now || Tells the currently playing song

+resume || Resumes the currently playing song

+stop || Stops the currently playing song and clears the queue

+skip || Vote to skip the currently playing song

+queue || Tells the current song queue

+shuffle || Shuffles the queue

+remove [song_url] || Removes a song from the queue

+loop || Loops the currently playing song
